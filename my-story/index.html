"My Story"

I was born in 1997 in Samut Prakan province.

I attended Wat Suan Som School from kindergarten to Grade 6 and continued my secondary education at Wisutthikasatree School.

After completing high school, due to various problems, I did not pursue further education at the university.

The beginning of working life.

After finishing my studies, I had to find a job, leading me to work in various roles since a young age.

I started as a salesperson in a department store,

then worked in a factory, and later became a bank officer.
